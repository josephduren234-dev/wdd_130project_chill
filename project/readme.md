# PulseAir Website Project

## Overview
PulseAir is a concept website for the world’s first party airline — where the seat-back screen is a DJ booth, the safety video is a banger, and there’s a buffet on the plane. The site answers one question: what if flying were actually fun? PulseAir is designed for college students, young travelers, and families who want more excitement and energy from their travel experience.

## Website Purpose
- Provide a clean, simple, and energetic interface for users to learn about PulseAir.
- Make fare booking easy and engaging.
- Showcase the unique features and vibe of PulseAir.

## Target Audience
- College students
- Families with young kids
- Anyone seeking a fun, eventful flying experience

## Main Features
- **Homepage:**
  - Brief description of PulseAir and its unique concept
  - Image of a PulseAir plane
  - Logo in the top left of the navigation bar
- **About Us Page:**
  - Description of the airline’s purpose and menu
  - Images of food served by the airline
  - Image of an airline employee
- **Fare Booking Page:**
  - Descriptions of three fare options
  - Icons for each fare
